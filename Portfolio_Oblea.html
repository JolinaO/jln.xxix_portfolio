ABOUT ME

I am Jolina Oblea, an aspiring multimedia artist with a strong foundation in digital illustration and photography, currently developing my skills in motion graphics and multimedia content creation. I have a keen eye for composition, color, and visual storytelling, and I enjoy translating ideas into clear, engaging visuals that communicate effectively across digital platforms. While I am still building technical experience in motion and video production, I am highly motivated to learn, receptive to feedback, and comfortable refining my work through iteration. I value growth through practice and collaboration and approach each project as an opportunity to improve both creatively and technically. Curious, adaptable, and detail-oriented, I am continuously exploring ways to transform static visuals into dynamic content and contribute meaningfully to creative teams in a professional media environment.
